## 🇺🇸 About

**Product Manager for Payments & Enablement Systems at [Insider Store](https://www.insiderstore.com.br/)** — a Brazilian tech-performance fashion DTC on Shopify Plus.

I came into product through an uncommon path: started in **FP&A**, evolved into **Treasury**, and moved to **Digital Product** when it became clear that the biggest value levers — auth rate, smart routing, fraud, cash cycle — live at the intersection of payments, systems, and financial operations.

Today I operate where few PMs transit: **from transaction approval to cash hitting the bank account**. I know the checkout side (gateways, 3DS, antifraud, conversion) and the money side (CNAB/OFX reconciliation, receivables, financial cycle).

Hands-on with Python, SQL, Power BI, n8n, and applied AI. I build solutions when building is the right call.

`Payments Performance` · `Gateways & Acquirers` · `Antifraud` · `Smart Routing` · `Checkout Conversion` · `Enablement Systems`
